Common Vulneralibities

Cross-site Scripting(XSS)

It is a critical Vulneralibity that can lead to executing javascript and load other scripts into your website. It is so dangerous that it can lead to Remote-Code-Execution (RCE). It can be in the form of Self-XSS, Stored-XSS, Reflected-XSS or DOM-Based-XSS.

SQL Injection

This is really dangerous attack because it can not only execute queries that can have an impact on your Database but also it Read, Write, Store and even delete Your whole Database, hence may lead to critical problems if some Evil-Hacker finds it. but Don't worry we got your Back!

File Upload Vulneralibities

When someone can upload anything to your server or site it can create a problem when that person uploaded an Exploit hence when that Exploit is triggered that person will get a Remote-Code-Execution(RCE).

Just Few Vulneralibities?

No there are whole bunch of Vulneralibities like click-jacking, Bypassing authentication and authorization, IDORs and many more!
